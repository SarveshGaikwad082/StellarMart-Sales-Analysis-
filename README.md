# StellarMart Sales Analysis 📊

### Interactive Excel Dashboard & Business Analysis Project

Comprehensive Excel-based sales analysis project focused on identifying business growth trends, regional performance, seasonal fluctuations, and category-wise insights for StellarMart during 2023–2024.

# 🎯 Business Problem

StellarMart, a mid-sized retail chain specializing in **Electronics, Clothing, Home Appliances, Books, and Groceries**, wanted to analyze two years of sales data to uncover:

- Sales growth opportunities
- Seasonal demand patterns
- Region-wise performance trends
- Underperforming categories
- High-performing products

The objective was to transform raw business data into actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

---

# 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance across:
- Regions
- Product categories
- Monthly & quarterly trends
- Top-performing products
- Year-over-Year growth

The dashboard was designed entirely in **Microsoft Excel** using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, and Interactive Slicers.

# 📌 Key Business Insights

| Analysis | Insight |
|---|---|
| YoY Growth | Sales increased by 3.99% in 2024 |
| Best Quarter | Q3 2024 showed highest growth |
| Weak Quarter | Q2 recorded a sales decline |
| Top Growth Region | East (+21.7%) |
| Declining Regions | South & West |
| Peak Sales Month | July |
| Lowest Sales Month | February |
| Best Selling Product | Jacket |

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve Q2 sales strategy with targeted promotions
- Focus recovery efforts in South & West regions
- Launch region-specific category campaigns
- Increase inventory before peak demand periods
- Strengthen underperforming product categories

---

# 🧹 Data Cleaning Process

- Removed duplicate and blank records
- Extracted Month, Quarter, and Year from dates
- Standardized product and category names
- Validated sales calculations
- Applied consistent formatting for reporting
